 [Summary]
 
  This is a freeware game. Run "StartWindowed" to play in a window, or "StartFullscreen" to play in fullscreen.
 
 
 
 [Basic Movement]
 
  Confirm: Z key
  Cancel: X key
  Open Menu: X key
  Move/Select: Arrow keys
  Return to Title: F12
